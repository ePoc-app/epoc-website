---
title: "Unlock Conditions"
description: "Guide to define unlocking conditions for pages and chapters in your scenario."
icon: "lucide:lock"
---

You can set unlocking conditions for each page or chapter. This allows you to control access to different parts of your ePoc based on the actions or performance of the learner.

To set an unlocking condition, follow these steps:

::steps{level=4}
#### Select the page or chapter for which you want to set a condition.

#### Click on the 'Configure conditions' button in the 'Unlock Conditions' section.

#### Click on the 'Add a condition' button.

#### Fill in the three fields from left to right:
::steps{level=5}
##### First field
Select an element of the educational scenario to apply a condition to it. Click on the element to which you want to apply the condition.

##### Second field
Choose the condition to apply to the selected element. For example, if you have chosen a video, you will have the choice between 'Played' or 'Watched'.

##### Third field
Define the value of the condition.
::
::
